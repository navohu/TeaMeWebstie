User Manual

This is guide for users of the application TeaMe which we have developed for ATOS. The instructions provided below are for using the application.


Register

By filling all the required fields with your details, click the register button at the end of the page, register with the application.

Log in

If you already have an account by registering earlier, you are able to log in. Fill in the 2 fields given with your username and password and click login button to log in the system.

Choose a team

You will see a set of teams you are already in, if you are not a new user. You can choose one of them to continue with. Otherwise, you can create a new team by clicking the plus button in the up right corner of the page.

Choose a feeling

You should choose one of the moods given in the next page each time you logged in. By selecting one of the emoji pictures you can provide you mood at the moment you log in.

User Page

After you choose a team, and select your mood, you are directed to user page where you are able to see you personal details, location, status and mood. From this page, you can go to edit your profile, choose another team, chat with everyone in the “Tea Room”, message different people, go to news feed, view all the other users or log out whenever you want.

Edit Profile

You can edit your details such as you location, status and feeling. By simple clicking ‘Edit Profile’, you go the page with different fields carrying your details, choose whichever you want, update and then save by clicking the button at the end of the page. Or go back to the previous page by clicking the back button on the top left corner of the current page in case you change your mind to not update details.

Create a new team

Go to the ‘Choose a team’ page, click the ‘+’ icon in the up right corner of the page. Now you are directed to a page with fields provided for a team and its members. Type the name you want to call the new team and then click create. Afterwards, type the names of the users in the fields given for the members of the team and click add each time you add a new user. Remember that the users you add have to be already registered with TeaMe.

Choose a team

You will see a set of teams you are already in, if you are not a new user. You can choose one of them to continue with. Otherwise, you can create a new team by clicking the plus button in the up right corner of the page.

Chat with others in the TeaRoom

The aim of this TeaRoom is to make the users more sociable. So, simply by going to the TeaRoom page you can have a chat with all the users of the application and socialize with them. Click the TeaRoom icon on the current page, go to the TeaRoom, type whatever you want in the message box provided below, and then click send to share your thoughts with everyone in the TeaRoom.

Post in the news feed

Click News Feed icon, and go to the news feed page of the team you are currently in. Now use the post box below of the page to write your post and click post to make your messages, news be seen by other members of the group.

View all users

Click the ‘View all users’ button in the top right corner in the TeaRoom, and now you are provided with a list of all the registered users currently in present in the TeaRoom at the same time.

Messaging

In the messaging page a user is able to pick one member of the team and chat with that person in a closed ‘room’ where no one else will have access.

Log Out

You can log out from the application any time you want. Just click Logout icon in on the top right corner of the page, and now you are logged out.
